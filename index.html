Live bus timetable are connected to the GPS of each bus. They also communicate through 3G so the live bus timetable board can get the latest information for example bus being cancelled or delayed. Passengers can check when is the bus that they are going to take will arrive from this board or from their phone app. Then they wouldn't have to wait at the bus stop the whole time but doing something else while waiting for the bus. Photo is taken at the bus stop in front of the Manner Street Burger King. Reference Link

The spark wifi zone is a part of the spark payphone service. It provides wifi within a distance of 50m. Users can choose from three wifi plans which are: Guest, Spark Broadband user and Spark mobile plan user. There are more than 1000 Spark Wifi Zone in New Zealand and the photo at the left shows one of them. Photo is taken in Courtney Place. Reference Link
